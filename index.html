A Legacy of Excellence in the Heart of Sahiwal

Welcome to **Govt. Graduate College of Commerce, Sahiwal**, an institution deeply rooted in the fertile soil of the Montgomery region. Just as Sahiwal is known for its rich agricultural heritage and the world-renowned Sahiwal cattle breed, our college is dedicated to nurturing the finest human resource in the fields of **Commerce, Finance, and Information Technology**.

Situated in a city known for its peace and educational fervor, GGCC Sahiwal has been a beacon of knowledge for over six decades. We pride ourselves on blending the traditional values of our local culture with the fast-paced demands of the global digital economy. Whether you are pursuing an **I.Com, ICS, or a specialized BS Degree**, you are joining a community committed to integrity, innovation, and industrial leadership.
