Quaid’s message

You must concentrate on gaining knowledge and education. It is your foremost responsibility. Political awareness of the era is also a part of your education. You must be aware of international events and environment. Without education, it is complete darkness and with education it is light. Education is a matter of life and death to our nation. The world is moving so fast that if you do not educate yourself, you will not only be completely left behind, but also be finished up. The Holy Prophet (ﷺ) has enjoined his followers to go even to China in the pursuit of knowledge.

Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah

Principal’s message

Government Graduate College of Commerce, Sahiwal is shining like a star in the galaxy of education. It has rich and exemplary traditions. I, being the custodian of this heritage, deem it my obligation to uphold and enhance the treasure of these golden traditions. All of my aspirations and endeavors are meant to inculcate zeal and zest in the new generation to face the odds of practical life courageously and daringly.

Our nation needs assiduous, honest and diligent youth — and this college is trying its utmost to prepare them on these lines with special focus on character building. The college environment is being made conducive and fascinating to enlighten the vision and widen the mental horizon of students and teachers. I have firm belief in the dedication and prudence of the faculty. Amalgamation of intellectual fertility of both teachers and students would bear laudable fruits.

I urge all staff and students to be disciplined and determined to uplift the standard of our nation in the comity of the world. Alhamdolillah — alongside the 4-year semester BS Commerce and BS Accounting & Finance and the 2-year semester ADP Commerce, we are planning to start BBA and new combinations at intermediate level such as F.A. (IT) and ICS with Statistics, Maths and Computer Science.

Prof. Muhammad Akram Saqib

Incharge Principal
