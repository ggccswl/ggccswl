BS Commerce — Eight semesters ▼

Semesters I–IV align with the ADP-style foundation (accounting, business, economics, English, stats, Islamic studies, law, cost, finance, auditing, etc.) as listed for ADP above.

Semester V: Management, Financial Management, Research Methods, Marketing, Business Ethics & CSR, AIS, Leadership & Community Development, Corporate Law & Secretarial Practices, Managerial Economics.

Semester VI: Managerial Accounting, HRM, plus governance and related papers.

Semester VII–VIII: Strategic Management, Entrepreneurship & SME, Business Taxation, International Business, E-commerce, electives, and internship / project with viva (6 credits) as per GCUF scheme.

A 5th-semester bridge track is documented in the prospectus for eligible students — see printed prospectus for exact codes.

BS Accounting & Finance — Eight semesters ▼

Years 1–2: Principles & financial accounting, intro to business, micro/macro economics, functional English, Pakistan Studies, business maths & statistics, Islamic studies, advanced accounting, business law, cost accounting, business finance, auditing, business taxation, corporate law, sociology, psychology, customs & sales, statistical inference.

Upper years: Management, marketing, corporate finance, audit & assurance, financial management, management accounting, HRM, financial reporting series, managerial economics, corporate governance, strategic management, entrepreneurship, investment analysis, financial risk management, Islamic banking & finance, econometrics, financial modelling, computerized accounting, advanced cost & management accounting, analysis of financial statements, international finance — plus internship/project per university rules.

D.Com & CCA (PBTE)

D.Com is offered in 1st and 2nd shift (2nd shift boys only, light blue shirt in summer per uniform rules). The six-month Certificate in Computer Applications (CCA) is offered under PBTE, Lahore. Detailed PBTE syllabi are issued by the board.
