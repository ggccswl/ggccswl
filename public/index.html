About the college

A legacy in the heart of Sahiwal

Welcome to Government Graduate College of Commerce, Sahiwal — from its beginnings in 1964 as a commercial training institute to today’s portfolio of intermediate, diploma, ADP, BS Commerce, and BS Accounting & Finance programs under semester rules at the New Campus.

The college develops responsibility, confidence, and professional skills through qualified faculty, many holding M.Phil. and Ph.D. degrees. We are affiliated with BISE Sahiwal for I.Com & ICS, PBTE Lahore for D.Com & CCA, and GCUF Faisalabad for ADP and BS programs.
