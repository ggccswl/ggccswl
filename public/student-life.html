Leave Applications

  • Submit on the prescribed form to the incharge attendance.
  • Must be signed by father/guardian.
  • Hostel residents apply through the warden/superintendent.
  • Incharge attendance sanctions leave; more than three days requires Principal approval.
  • Sick leave over six days needs a medical certificate from a certified practitioner.
  • Late applications are not entertained.
  • Scholarship holders may lose scholarship for the absence period.
  • Board exams: 80% lectures required; university exams: 75%.

Attendance & House Tests

Attendance is monitored daily. 80% attendance is required for D.Com / I.Com / ICS; 75% for ADC and BS classes to forward admission for examinations.

Monthly class tests are recorded; weightage is given when sending admission for board/university exams. Students below 50% in class tests and send-up may be treated as failed and not allowed to sit board/university annual exams. Parents may review monthly test and attendance reports. Position holders receive medals, prizes, and certificates at the annual prize distribution (typically March/April).

Fee Concession & Scholarships

The college offers fee concession to deserving students at admission - from full to half fee depending on case.

Government and other scholarships (apply annually as notified):

  • Benevolent Fund
  • Ministry of Religious Affairs Scholarship
  • BISE Sahiwal Merit Scholarships
  • PBTE Lahore Merit Scholarships
  • Fauji Foundation Scholarships
  • Student Aid Society
  • Zakat
  • Red Crescent Scholarships
  • Additional Schemes as announced

Students are eligible for Chief Minister Laptop Scheme, Honahaar Scholarship, and similar government initiatives; many students have received laptops and Honhaar awards in past years.

Parent–Teacher Meetings

A Parent–Teacher association supports regular meetings so families stay informed about progress and welfare.

Alumni Association

Old students meet regularly; the association supports college welfare projects when needed.

College Council

Senior faculty, philanthropists, and city dignitaries guide major decisions on standards, property, and welfare. Council decisions are final as per prospectus.

Security Refund & ID Cards

Security fee is refunded on successful completion of the course. Every student must carry the college ID card during college hours.
