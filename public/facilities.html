Facilities & student experience

Academic support, health, residence, sports, and character development as described in the prospectus.

📚

Library

A historic, well-stocked library offers course books and unique volumes across many subjects. It supports research, quiet study, and wider reading beyond the classroom.

💻

Computer laboratories

Two computer labs — one at the Old Campus and one at the New Campus — with modern equipment to deliver contemporary IT and business software skills.

🏠

Hostel

On-campus hostel accommodation is available under published rules. A warden and superintendent manage operations; students apply on a separate form.

⛑️

Dispensary

A college dispensary provides first aid for students who fall ill or need immediate assistance on campus.

Sports

The college supports participation at local, divisional, and provincial levels. Teams compete in PBTE, GCUF Faisalabad, and inter-board events. The college has earned overall first position in PBTE sports and continues to win positions across disciplines — building fitness, teamwork, and discipline.

Co-curricular activities

Year-round activities include debates, seminars, Qirat and Naat competitions, character-building sessions, and speech contests on diverse topics. A dedicated tutorial period supports these goals.

Character Building Society

Senior faculty and community notables work throughout the year to strengthen ethics, morale, and civic responsibility among students.

Educational & recreational visits

Students take part in tours to places of educational interest to deepen understanding and confidence. Participation is encouraged to broaden perspective beyond the classroom.
