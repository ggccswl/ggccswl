Admission Overview

Admission is granted on merit, once a year, according to schedules set by the University / Boards and advertised in local and national media. Separate applications are required for each discipline where applicable.

ADP / BS — Rules

  • Intermediate or equivalent from any board;
  • domicile of Punjab only.
  • 20 marks for Hafiz-e-Quran where applicable.
  • Age limit 20 years for ADP/BS; Principal may grant up to 4 years relaxation.
  • Candidates must appear before the interview committee with original documents and fee.
  • No right of admission if the committee / Principal finds a candidate unfit or disruptive at interview.
  • Separate application for each discipline.
  • 3% quota for persons with disabilities and 5% quota
  • vacant reserved seats convert to open merit.

I.Com / ICS / D.Com / F.A (IT) - Rules

  • Matriculation Certificate required. ICS (Matric with Science). I.Com / D.Com (Matric with Arts/Science.
  • Candidates should have passed the Annual Exams of the current year or 2nd Annual of the previous year.
  • No NOC is required for admission in D.Com
  • NOC is required from concerned board for admission in I.Com / ICS if not passed from BISE Sahiwal.

BS Programs & Examinations

BS (Commerce) and BS (Accounting & Finance) are Four-Year (8 Semester) Programs.

Mid-Term Exams are conducted internally by the college; Final Exams are conducted by GCUF Faisalabad.

Internship & Practical Training

After BS completion, students must complete 8 weeks’ internship in a public/private limited company or financial institution and submit an internship report examined by internal and external examiners. The result card is issued after qualifying viva voce conducted by the university.

Documents for Admission

Use only the official prescribed form with printed serial number — photocopies of the form are not accepted.

  1. Intermediate Result Card (4 Copies) for admission in BS Program;
  2. Matric Result Card (4 Copies) for admission in Intermediate Programs.
  3. Character Certificate from the head of the last institution (2 copies).
  4. Four Colour Passport Size Photographs with sky blue background (1 attested on front, 3 attested on back.
  5. Two Colour 1×1 inch Size Photographs, with sky blue background, without attestation.
  6. Computerized CNIC (B-Form) 2 copies each.
  7. NOC (original) if not from BISE Sahiwal.
  8. Domicile of Punjab (2 copies).
  9. Health Proforma attached with Admission Form, attested by registered medical practitioner.
