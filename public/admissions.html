Admission overview

Admission is granted on merit, once a year, according to schedules set by the University / Boards and advertised in local and national media. Separate applications are required for each discipline where applicable.

ADP / BS — rules

  • Intermediate or equivalent from any board; domicile of Punjab only.
  • 20 marks for Hafiz-e-Quran where applicable.
  • Age limit 20 years for ADP/BS; Principal may grant up to 4 years relaxation.
  • Candidates must appear before the interview committee with original documents and fee.
  • No right of admission if the committee / Principal finds a candidate unfit or disruptive at interview.
  • Separate application for each discipline.
  • 3% quota for persons with disabilities and 5% for minorities; vacant reserved seats convert to open merit.

I.Com / ICS / D.Com / F.A. etc.

  • Matriculation pass required. ICS: matric with science. I.Com / D.Com: matric arts or science.
  • Candidates should have passed the annual exams of the current year or 2nd annual of the previous year.
  • No NOC required for D.Com from other boards; NOC from concerned board required for I.Com / ICS if not passed from BISE Sahiwal.

BS programs & examinations

BS Commerce and BS Accounting & Finance are four-year, eight-semester programs. Mid-term exams are conducted internally by the college; final exams are conducted by GCUF Faisalabad.

Internship & practical training

After BS completion, students must complete 8 weeks’ internship in a public/private limited company or financial institution and submit an internship report examined by internal and external examiners. The result card is issued after qualifying viva voce conducted by the university.

Documents for ADP/BS forms

Use only the official prescribed form with printed serial number — photocopies of the form are not accepted.

  1. Intermediate (D.Com / I.Com / ICS / B.A / F.A / F.Sc / A-Level) result card — 4 copies for BS; Matric certificate/result — 4 copies for intermediate programs.
  2. Character certificate from the head of the last institution — 2 copies.
  3. Four colour passport photos (sky blue background): 1 attested on front, 3 on back.
  4. Two colour photos 1×1 inch, sky blue background, without attestation.
  5. Computerized CNIC: self and father/guardian (B-Form acceptable for inter) — 2 copies each.
  6. NOC (original) if not from BISE Sahiwal where required.
  7. Domicile (Punjab only) — 2 copies.
  8. Health proforma attached with form, attested by registered medical practitioner — original.
