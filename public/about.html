Introduction

Government Graduate College of Commerce, Sahiwal

Prospectus 2025–26 — history, campuses, and educational mission.

Origins & upgrades

The college was established in 1964 as Government Commercial Training Institute to impart commerce and business education up to higher secondary (D.Com). It was upgraded for B.Com in 1986, and upgraded to postgraduate M.Com level in 2003 given strong results and performance.

I.Com and ICS were introduced in 2014 and 2015. In 2019, B.Com was replaced with the Associate Degree in Commerce (ADP), and the college received affiliation for four-year BS programs in Commerce and Accounting & Finance.

A six-month Certificate in Computer Applications (CCA) offered by PBTE Lahore began in 2020.

Programs today

  • Old Campus: 6-month CCA; 2-year D.Com, I.Com & ICS (1st shift); D.Com 2nd shift (boys only).
  • New Campus: 2-year ADP and 4-year BS (Commerce & Accounting & Finance), semester system, for boys and girls on a regular basis.
  • Intermediate options in the prospectus also include F.A. (IT) and multiple ICS combinations (Maths/Eco, Stats/Eco, Stats/Maths) — see course outlines.

Heritage building (Old Campus)

The Old Campus building was constructed in 1935 as D.A.V. High School. The foundation stone was laid on 2 April 1935 by Malik Sir Feroz Khan Noon, Minister for Education at the time. It was built in memory of Rai Bahadur Lal Ganga Ram Sahib and Lal Bhagwan Das Sahib of Pakpattan by their sons.

The college first operated from a rented building in 1964, then moved to the present Evacuee Trust Property Department building. The property (18 kanals, 12 marlas, 5 sarsahi) was later transferred to the college, where a new B.Com block was built in 1986.

New Campus & land

To meet public and industry demand, the government allocated 51 kanals, 0 marlas, 8 sarsahi where the hostel and postgraduate block were constructed in 2003–04 — known as the New Campus.

Total land under college control is approximately 69 kanals, 13 marlas, 4 sarsahi.

Motto & faculty

The college fosters responsibility, confidence, commitment, and devotion to the professions by grooming personalities and combining vision, resourcefulness, and initiative. It aims to supply technically trained human resources for the region.

Faculty includes highly qualified Professors and Lecturers, many with M.Phil. and Ph.D. credentials. Departmental listings appear on the faculty page.
